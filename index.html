COBALT

CLARITY-Optimized Brain anALysis Tools.

Tissue clearing methods, such as CLARITY, have enabled researchers to image the whole brain intact using light sheet microscopy, preserving connections between regions. We hope to drive breakthrough neuroscience discoveries by enabling multi-terabyte (TB) cleared brain tissue analysis.
